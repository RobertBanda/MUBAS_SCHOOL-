The best learning methods

At Magodi Private Secondary School, we are committed to academic excellence, discipline, and holistic student development. Our online registration system has been designed to provide parents with a simple, secure, and transparent way to apply for admission. Through this platform, parents can submit their child’s bio-data, track application progress, and receive official feedback from the school administration. We believe in efficiency, accountability, and clear communication as we prepare learners for a successful future.

Outstanding Student Achievement

Magodi Private Secondary School continues to produce excellent academic results. We are proud to announce that, according to the selection list released on 16 March 2025, more than 17 of our students were selected to various universities and colleges. This remarkable achievement reflects our commitment to academic excellence, discipline, and quality teaching. Our dedicated teachers and hardworking students consistently strive for high performance, ensuring that our learners are well prepared for higher education and future careers.

    Academic Strengthening Initiative

    14 April 2016 Area 23 Tambalare

    At Magodi Private Secondary School, we have introduced a structured academic reinforcement program designed to improve examination performance. At the end of every topic, students are required to complete written exercises and revision tasks to deepen their understanding and strengthen their exam preparation. We also encourage parents to ensure that students bring their previous examination papers and marked scripts when reporting to school. These papers help teachers identify areas of weakness and provide targeted support. Through continuous writing practice, revision, and strong parent–teacher cooperation, we aim to produce well-prepared and high-performing students by the end of Form 4. (...)
